We Print Lanyards

Photoshop • Illustrator • Quark • Procreate

Jo is currently the Product Designer and Graphic artist at We Print Lanyards located in Long Eaton, East Midlands. Their primary role is designing stock lanyards, along with assisting the artworking team and marketing team.

Christmas
