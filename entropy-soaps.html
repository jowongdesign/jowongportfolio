Entropy Soaps

InDesign • Photoshop

This was their Master's project, where they created a series of soap packaging that would subvert gendered and gender neutral packaging on health and beauty products. At the time they were interested in making artisanal soaps and gender studies. They believe that ones gender expression is unique to them and that it there is a lot of potential in having expressive packaging.
