The Medium is the Message

Collaging

A term coined by Marshall McLuhan, to mean that the form of the message (print, visual, musical, etc.) determines the way the message is perceived. This was a typography project where they had to convey this message through their own medium.
They made theirs into a booklet with the phrase collaged onto it. They printed out this phrase and constructed a message through it.
