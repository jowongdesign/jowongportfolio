The Medium is the Message

Collaging

A term coined by Marshall McLuhan, to mean that the form of the message (print, visual, musical, etc.) determines the way the message is perceived. This was a typography project where we had to convey this message through our own medium.
I made mine into a booklet with the phrase collaged onto it. As I was utilising the print outs of the words the more messages I found in it.
