About

In 3 seconds

Jo is a creative practitioner and part time megalomaniac.

In 15 seconds

Jo is a non-binary graphic designer and visual artist that works with both digital and print design. They have a M.A. in Design and B.A. in Interactive Design. They also have a post-graduate diploma in Gender Studies. Their work has been displayed at the University of Lincoln Art Show in 2017 and 2019. They are currently working for We Print Lanyards as a Product Designer and Graphic Artist.

Beyond Design

They are passionate about social justice, nerd culture, and skincare. In their free time they run an online community for queer people: for discussions about politics, sharing memes, and game nights on Saturday evenings.
